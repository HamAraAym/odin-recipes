Butter Chicken Masala

Description

This butter chicken recipe, or chicken makhani, is one of my favorite Indian dishes. It features a full-flavored sauce that complements the chicken well. It can be made as mild or spicy as you wish by adjusting the cayenne. Serve with basmati rice and naan bread.

Butter Chicken Masala, also known as Chicken Makhani, is a beloved Indian dish featuring tender chicken cooked in a rich, creamy tomato-based sauce. The sauce is flavored with a blend of spices, including garam masala, cumin, and turmeric, creating a deliciously complex taste. This versatile dish can be adjusted to suit your spice preference and is best enjoyed with basmati rice and naan bread for a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 shallot finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-size pieces
